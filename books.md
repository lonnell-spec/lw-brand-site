# Books — Lonnell Williams

## Anything, Everything, or Nothing at All
**Status:** Fall 2026 (Re-release)
**Genre:** Ministry Leadership / Theology

Six words changed everything. On a ministry trip, a resource developer answered a simple question with: "Anything, everything, or nothing at all." That phrase became the heartbeat of this book — and a standard that has no room for halfway. Whether you're sitting in the pew, running a campus, or leading a staff of fifty, this is the book that names what's at stake when you decide to serve God's vision for real.

## The Staff: What's Already in Your Hand
**Status:** In Development
**Genre:** Ministry Leadership / Operations

Most leaders spend their entire ministry looking for better resources, better staff, better tools — when the most powerful thing they have is already in their hand. The Staff is a reckoning with what you've been given. A reframe of what "enough" looks like when you actually deploy it. This is the operator's guide to building with what's available, not what's ideal.
