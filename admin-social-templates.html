Canva Tips
  • Brand Kit: Add all 6 hex codes + 3 fonts. Set Obsidian as default background.
  • Ghost Text: Add huge Thunder text, color same as background, drop opacity to 3–6%. Position off-canvas edges.
  • Sidebar Stripe: Rectangle element, 16px wide, full height, snap to left edge, Copper.
  • Photo Templates: Add photo in upper half. Add black rectangle over it, transparency 85%. Bring type to front.
  • Scan Lines: Search "line texture" in Canva elements, or create repeating thin rectangles at 2–3% opacity.
  • Batch Workflow: Duplicate templates. Change only the text. Export all at once as PNG. 10 posts in 20 minutes.
Adobe Express Tips
  • Brand Kit: Add 6 colors + 3 fonts under Brand → Brand Kit.
  • Resize: Design at 1080×1350, then use Resize for Stories (1080×1920) and Square (1080×1080).
  • Ghost Text: Same approach — large text element, low opacity, behind main content layer.

Saturday Batch Production
  • Time: 60–90 minutes total
  • Process: Open Canva → load templates → pull 4 statements from content bank → drop into templates → swap photos on T2/T5 → export as PNG → schedule
  • Output: 4 IG posts + resize to 1080×1920 for Stories = 8 pieces in ~20 minutes once templates are built
  • Challenge Series: Budget 15 extra minutes once a month for 01 Annotated (the handwriting layer takes care) and 04 Data Stat (verify the stat before posting)
