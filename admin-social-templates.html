BOLD TYPE TEMPLATES.

Overview

8 typography-forward templates for Instagram and Threads. Built on the LW brand identity system — Bebas Neue, Obsidian, Copper, Signal Red. Each template is designed to be recreated in Canva or Adobe Express using the exact specs below.

The 2026 typography trend is neo-brutalist: oversized type, raw layouts, intentional friction, editorial minimalism. Your brand already has this DNA. These templates are the execution layer.

Typography

RoleFontSize (Canva)Rules
HeadlinesBebas Neue120–220ptALWAYS all caps. Letter spacing: +50 to +100
Body CopyDM Sans Light28–36ptWeight 300. Line height 1.5. Mixed case.
Labels / MetadataSpace Mono16–20ptLetter spacing +300 to +500. ALL CAPS.
AttributionSpace Mono14–16ptLetter spacing +400. Copper color. Bottom of layout.

Layout Elements

ElementSpec
Safe Zone / Padding100px on all sides — no text in outer 100px
Sidebar Stripe16px wide, left edge, full height, Copper
Top Bar12px tall, full width, Signal Red or gradient (Signal → Copper)
Accent Line60px × 4px, Signal Red, above headline
Ghost TextBebas Neue at 500–700pt, opacity 3–6%, single word bleeding off edges
Scan Lines (texture)Horizontal lines, 1px, 4–5px gap, 2–3% opacity over Copper/Signal BGs
Photo OverlayLinear gradient: transparent → #0D0D0D at 85–98% opacity. Start at 50–60% from top.

Canva Pro Tips

  • Brand Kit: Add all 5 hex codes + 3 fonts. Set Obsidian as default background.
  • Ghost Text: Add huge Bebas Neue text, color same as background, drop opacity to 3–6%. Position off-canvas edges.
  • Sidebar Stripe: Rectangle element, 16px wide, full height, snap to left edge, Copper.
  • Photo Templates: Add photo in upper half. Add black rectangle over it, transparency 85%. Bring type to front.
  • Scan Lines: Search “line texture” in Canva elements, or create repeating thin rectangles at 2–3% opacity.
  • Batch Workflow: Duplicate templates. Change only the text. Export all at once as PNG. 10 posts in 20 minutes.

Adobe Express Tips

  • Brand Kit: Add 5 colors + 3 fonts under Brand → Brand Kit.
  • Resize: Design at 1080×1350, then use Resize for Stories (1080×1920) and Square (1080×1080).
  • Ghost Text: Same approach — large text element, low opacity, behind main content layer.
