THE BLOCK LETTERS
Directly inspired by your AENA logo. Four bold block letters in the palette grid — cyan A, red E, yellow N, teal A. Playful, graphic, immediately recognizable. Album-cover energy. Maximum shelf impact.

THE STACKED TYPE
Each word on its own line in a different AENA color. Bold, modern display type. Subtle color glows in the background. The title becomes a visual cascade — anything (cyan), everything (yellow), nothing (teal). Red commas as punctuation accents.

THE MANIFESTO
Three bold color stripes — each carrying one word. Cyan ANYTHING, yellow EVERYTHING, teal NOTHING. Black bottom section with "at all." Feels like a protest poster, an album drop, a statement. Maximum visual disruption. Nobody walks past this on a shelf.
