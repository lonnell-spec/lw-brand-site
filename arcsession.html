03 · The Process

The 6A.
Every System Runs Through It.

Six verbs. Pulled from Acts 6. Every system — external or internal — installs through these six steps. Miss one, it breaks. Run all six, it ships.

— 01 · Acts 6:1
Analyze
What is actually breaking — what is the root cause?
— 02 · Acts 6:2
Assess
What can only I do? What is the engine? What is non-negotiable?
— 03 · Acts 6:3
Assign
Who has the character, Spirit, and wisdom to own this?
— 04 · Acts 6:2,6
Attest
Does the whole team know, publicly, that this is happening?
— 05 · Acts 6:5
Affirm
Do the people affected actually trust the person carrying it?
— 06 · Acts 6:7
Acknowledge
At 90 days — what worked? What needs adjusting?
