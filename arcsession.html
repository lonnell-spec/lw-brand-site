02 · Acts 6 — The Core Insight

The Table And The
Pulpit Are The Same
Ministry.

"Serve tables" — diakonein trapezais. "Ministry of the word" — tē diakonia tou logou. Same root. Same word. Luke is making a claim we've been ignoring for centuries.

03 · The Framework

The B.U.I.L.D.
Protocol.

Acts 6 is not just a story — it's a protocol. Five moves, in order. Miss one, the whole thing breaks. Run all five, Acts 6:7 is your outcome.

B
Break The Silence
Name the gap publicly. The gap is not evidence of failure. The gap is the assignment.
U
Uncompromise The Engine
Know the three things only you can do. Prayer. The Word. Vision. Refuse to get pulled off them.
I
Identify Your Seven
Not the most available. The most qualified. Good repute. Full of Spirit. Full of wisdom.
L
Launch Publicly
Hands on. In front of the church. Private delegation creates silent confusion. Public commissioning creates covenant clarity.
D
Deploy The Domain
Acts 6:7 — "the word of God continued to increase." Fix the plumbing, the house expands.

05 · The Mechanic

The Monday
Morning Move.

Sunday is forty-eight hours behind you, five days ahead of you. One window. Don't leave this conference with notes. Leave with a meeting on the calendar.

The Ask
Book ninety minutes
with your Senior Pastor.

The 90-Minute Core vs. Tables Audit. Eight moves. One sheet of paper. One vertical line.

Pull Both Calendars

Last four weeks. Every recurring meeting, every decision, every standing block.

One Sheet. One Vertical Line.

Left column: ENGINE — prayer, Word, vision, preaching, shepherding, culture. Right column: TABLES — everything else.

Mark Every Item E Or T

No third category. No "a little bit of both." Make the call.

Protect The Engine

Every E on SP's calendar stays. Every T is a transfer target. Every T on XP's calendar transfers down to a qualified Seven.

Find The Longest-Stuck T

The top three items stuck longest on SP's plate. Not most-annoying. Longest-stuck.

Pick One. Just One.

That's your ninety-day transfer.

Commission Publicly

At the next staff meeting. Hands on. Prayer. Authority. Not a forwarded email.

Run It Again In Ninety Days

Same sheet. Different items. Every quarter until SP's calendar is 80% engine and yours is 80% system.

Text Your Senior Pastor Now
"I can protect what you cannot delegate.
But only if you will name it.
Acts 6. Ninety minutes. Monday."
