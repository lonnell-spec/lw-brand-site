Official Bio

LONNELL WILLIAMS

Builder · Operator · Author

Lonnell Williams serves as the Executive Pastor of 2819 Church in Atlanta, Georgia, under the leadership of Senior Pastor Philip Anthony Mitchell. In this role, he oversees the day-to-day operations of one of the fastest-growing churches in the Southeast, having scaled the organization from 13 to 50 staff members in six months.

A graduate of Princeton Theological Seminary (M.Div.), Lonnell is the author of Zag: Leading Ministry Jesus's Way and the forthcoming Anything, Everything, or Nothing at All. His work sits at the intersection of operational leadership, technology, and pastoral ministry.

As a speaker, Lonnell brings a confrontational, psychologically penetrating style that moves rooms from inspiration to execution. His signature topics include organizational scaling, counter-cultural leadership, and building infrastructure that outlasts the builder.
