Speaker Bio

OFFICIAL BIO.

Lonnell Williams is the Executive Pastor of 2819 Church in Atlanta, Georgia, where he oversees day-to-day operations for one of the city's fastest-growing congregations. He scaled the church staff from 13 to 50 in six months and is currently leading the launch of a second campus in South Atlanta.

A graduate of Princeton Theological Seminary (M.Div.) and a former Account Executive at Cisco/Splunk, Lonnell operates at the intersection of ministry and business — building infrastructure for what doesn't exist yet.

He is the author of "Zag: Leading Ministry Jesus's Way" and speaks nationally on leadership, operations, and organizational design. His conviction: I'd rather be an excellent #2 than an average #1.
