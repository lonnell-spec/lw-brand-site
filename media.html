Short Bio

Lonnell Williams is an author, operator, and Executive Pastor serving under Senior Pastor Philip Anthony Mitchell at 2819 Church in Atlanta, Georgia. He holds a Master of Divinity from Princeton Theological Seminary.

As the architect of operational infrastructure that scaled an organization from 13 to 50 staff members in six months, Lonnell specializes in systems building, leadership development, and the intersection of technology and ministry. He is the author of Anything, Everything, or Nothing at All and the forthcoming Zag: Leading Ministry Jesus's Way.
