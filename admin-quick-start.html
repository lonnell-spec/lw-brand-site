HOW THE SITE WORKS
1
Notion Is the CMS
Photos and Content Hub items are managed in Notion. When you add or update content in the Notion databases, the site syncs automatically every 15 minutes. You never need to touch code.
2
Instagram Syncs via Zapier
Every new Instagram post fires a Zapier webhook → Supabase → homepage grid. No manual work. Just post to Instagram as normal.
3
YouTube Syncs Every 15 Minutes
YouTube videos pull directly from the YouTube API on a 15-minute cron. New videos appear on the site automatically after they go live on YouTube.
4
Forms Route to Supabase
Newsletter signups, speaking inquiries, and consulting inquiries all route to the newsletter_subscribers, speaking_inquiries, and consulting_inquiries tables. View them in the admin portal.
COMMON TASKS
→
Add a photo to the gallery
Go to Notion → Photos DB → Add a row with the photo URL, caption, and category. It will appear on the site within 15 minutes.
→
View new speaking inquiries
Go to Admin Portal → Speaking Inquiries. All form submissions appear there in real time.
→
View newsletter subscribers
Go to Admin Portal → Newsletter Subscribers. Shows all email signups with source and timestamp.
→
Check YouTube sync status
Go to Admin Portal → YouTube. Shows all synced videos and last sync time.

Important: The admin portal is protected by sessionStorage. To log in, visit /admin.html and enter the portal password. Your session lasts until you close the browser tab.
