# Lonnell Williams

## Who He Is

Lonnell Williams is an Executive Pastor, author, speaker, and ministry operator based in Atlanta, Georgia. He serves as Executive Pastor at 2819 Church — a fast-growing congregation led by Senior Pastor Philip Anthony Mitchell — where he oversees operations, staff, multi-campus strategy, and organizational systems.

## Background

- Princeton Theological Seminary, M.Div.
- Former Account Executive at Cisco and Splunk (Fortune 500 enterprise sales)
- Executive Pastor at 2819 Church, Atlanta (2019–present)
- Author of multiple ministry leadership books
- Builder of AI-powered operational infrastructure for church organizations

## What He Does

Lonnell sits at the intersection of corporate operations, ministry leadership, and emerging technology. He has scaled 2819 Church from 13 to 50+ staff, managed eight-figure budgets, and built one of the most operationally sophisticated AI infrastructure stacks in ministry leadership — including a 42-agent AI Chief of Staff ecosystem.

He writes and speaks on:
- Ministry systems and operational infrastructure
- AI and technology for church leaders
- Executive leadership and organizational culture
- The theology of full commitment and calling
