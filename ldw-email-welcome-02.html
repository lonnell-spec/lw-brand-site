More specifically — you're probably one of these three:

The Church Leader
Senior pastor, executive pastor, ministry director, church planter. The organization is growing. The systems aren't keeping up. You've been to the conferences. You've read the books. The Christian Executive
C-suite or senior leadership, for-profit or nonprofit. You're serious about your faith and serious about your work. You've spent years feeling like the two require different languages, different rooms, different versions of yourself. You're done with that.

An entrepreneur, a founder, an operator motivated by calling as much as opportunity. Most entrepreneurial content ignores your faith entirely. Most faith content assumes you lead a church. You're in neither space fully.
