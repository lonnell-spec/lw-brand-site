7 PILLARS.
HONEST SCORES.

Each pillar includes a self-assessment rubric, a score, and a specific action if you're below the threshold.

01
VISION CLARITY
Does your team know where you're going and why? Clarity that stays in leadership doesn't move organizations.
02
STAFF HEALTH
Culture score, burnout risk, and communication health. The people are the ministry.
03
SYSTEMS & OPS
Are you running on documented processes or tribal knowledge? One departure shouldn't break anything.
04
FINANCIAL HEALTH
Runway, cash flow awareness, and giving trends. You can't build on a shaky financial foundation.
05
GUEST EXPERIENCE
From parking to follow-up — what does a first-time guest actually experience at your church?
06
LEADERSHIP DEV
Are you growing the next generation of leaders inside the house, or always recruiting from outside?
07
DIGITAL PRESENCE
Your digital footprint is your first impression. Online presence, content, and discoverability score.
