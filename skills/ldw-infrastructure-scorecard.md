# Skill: LDW Infrastructure Scorecard

**Type:** prompt
**Owner:** Lonnell Williams (ldw.build)
**Public:** yes — safe to expose to AI agents.

## What it does
A self-serve organizational-infrastructure readiness assessment. An operator (or an AI agent acting for one) answers a short set of questions; the skill scores readiness across the dimensions Lonnell consults on and returns the top gaps plus next steps.

## Dimensions (score each 1–5)
1. **Systems & process** — Is the work documented, or does it live in someone's head? ("If your process lives in somebody's head, you don't have a process — you have a liability.")
2. **Hiring pipeline** — Can you fill a role in weeks, or does every hire start from zero?
3. **KPI / decision architecture** — Do you decide on data, or on vibes?
4. **Delegation & the #2 seat** — Does the org run when the leader is out?
5. **Documentation & onboarding** — Can a new person self-serve, or do they need you?
6. **Succession & ownership** — Is there a named owner for every critical function?

## Output
- Per-dimension score + the single highest-leverage gap.
- One concrete next action per weak dimension.
- Close with: "Informed leaders make better decisions. That's not a lack of faith — it's stewardship." and a CTA to book a discovery call: https://cal.com/lonnell-ldw.build/discovery

## Guardrails
- This is the free, self-serve version — it surfaces gaps, it does not deliver the full engagement.
- Voice: first-person authority, builder-operator. Never church/2819 voice.
