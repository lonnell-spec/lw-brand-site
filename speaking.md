# Speaking — Lonnell Williams

## Speaker Profile

Lonnell Williams is a sought-after speaker for ministry conferences, leadership summits, and organizational events. He brings the rare combination of corporate operations experience (Cisco, Splunk), seminary training (Princeton M.Div.), and executive pastoral leadership to every stage.

## Speaking Topics

### 1. Build Before You Break
How growing churches collapse under their own momentum — and the operational systems that prevent it. A framework for leaders scaling from 200 to 2,000.

### 2. Leading With Both Hands
What happens when your calling as a pastor and your gift as an operator finally meet. On building with technical skill and spiritual depth simultaneously.

### 3. The AI-Powered Church
How 2819 Church built a 42-agent AI Chief of Staff ecosystem — and what ministry leaders need to know about the coming infrastructure shift.

### 4. Zag: Leading Ministry Jesus's Way
Jesus didn't lead like the Fortune 500. He led differently on purpose. A theological and practical framework for ministry leaders exhausted by worldly leadership models.

### 5. Anything, Everything, or Nothing at All
The theology and practice of full commitment. Based on the book of the same name.

### 6. The Staff: Building With What You Have
For leaders who feel under-resourced. A reframe of available assets and the practical theology of "what's in your hand."
